# Case Study: Cybersecurity Governance Framework for a Fintech Startup

## Scenario Overview

A growing fintech startup based in Greece with 100 employees had recently 
expanded into online payments. The CEO was concerned about cybersecurity 
threats and regulatory compliance, but the organisation lacked a formal 
governance framework.

## Objective

To design a practical, scalable cybersecurity governance framework tailored 
to the organisation's size, sector, and regulatory environment.

## Approach

The engagement covered five key areas:

- **Framework Selection** — Recommending appropriate international standards 
including ISO 27001, NIST CSF, PCI DSS, and GDPR based on the organisation's 
profile and obligations
- **Governance Structure** — Defining clear roles and responsibilities across 
all stakeholders from board level to individual employees
- **Priority Policies** — Identifying the three most critical policies for 
year one implementation and justifying their prioritisation
- **Performance Measurement** — Designing KPIs and metrics to track 
framework effectiveness over time
- **Executive Engagement** — Outlining strategies to secure board-level 
buy-in and embed a culture of ongoing compliance
